For whom

total area

60 square meters

location

Odessa

deadline

This apartment for a young person has a lot of concrete, brick and natural wood. This is a loft in the classical sense and with very interesting details. Quite a lot of different materials and textures are used in the interior. However, due to the fact that each material is used more than once and finds its continuation in other rooms, the apartment looks like a solid volume, where all the textures complement each other and are combined with each other.
